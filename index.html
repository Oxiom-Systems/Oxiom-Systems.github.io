Flagship product

TraceWeaver is the control layer for coding-agent work.

TraceWeaver is open-source today. It gives teams a file-based systems-engineering workflow for intent capture, requirements authority, task capsules, traceability checks, verification evidence, validation records, and controlled publication handoffs.
