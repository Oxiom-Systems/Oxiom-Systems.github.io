The framework

TraceWeaver is the loom between prompt and proof.

I

Intent

Ideas stay visible as stakeholder intent before an agent turns them into plans, code, tests, or release claims.

II

Authority

Meaningful behavior must point to an approved requirement or approved exception before it is treated as done.

III

Evidence

Verification and validation records show what changed, why it was allowed, and whether the original need is still satisfied.
